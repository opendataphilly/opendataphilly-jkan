  developed territory, encompassing residential, commercial, and other nonresidential

  density and urban land use resulting in a representation of the &quot;urban footprint.&quot;
  There are two types of urban areas: urbanized areas (UAs) that contain 50,000 or
  more people and urban clusters (UCs) that contain at least 2,500 people, but fewer
  than 50,000 people (except in the U.S. Virgin Islands and Guam which each contain
